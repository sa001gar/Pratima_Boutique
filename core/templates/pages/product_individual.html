Care Instructions

Dry Clean Only

Professional dry cleaning is recommended to maintain the silk's luster and intricate embroidery work.

Avoid Direct Sunlight

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading.

Proper Storage

Fold carefully with acid-free tissue paper or hang on padded hangers to maintain shape.

Maintenance Tips

Long-term Care

  • • Air the saree occasionally to prevent mustiness
  • • Use mothballs or neem leaves to protect from insects
  • • Avoid contact with perfumes and deodorants
  • • Handle with clean, dry hands to avoid stains
  • • Professional cleaning every 6-8 wears recommended

From Cocoon to Couture

The journey begins with the finest mulberry silk cocoons, carefully selected for their quality and luster. The silk is then dyed using traditional methods with natural colors that have been used for centuries.

The deep burgundy color you see is achieved through a complex process involving natural indigo and madder root, creating a rich, lasting hue that improves with age. This eco-friendly dyeing process ensures the colors remain vibrant for generations.
