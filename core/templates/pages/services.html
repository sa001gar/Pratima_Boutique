Fabric Care Guide

Preserve the beauty and longevity of your bespoke pieces with our expert care recommendations

Silk Care

  • Dry clean only for best results
  • Store in breathable garment bags
  • Avoid direct sunlight exposure
  • Iron on low heat with pressing cloth

Cotton Care

  • Machine wash in cold water
  • Use mild detergent
  • Air dry in shade
  • Iron while slightly damp

Embellished Care

  • Professional dry cleaning only
  • Store flat or padded hangers
  • Avoid contact with jewelry
  • Handle with clean hands
